About Me

My journey into programming began about 5 years ago. Rather than following a rigid traditional path, my learning has been deeply practical and organic—learning and building on a project-by-project basis through dedicated periods of self-study.

During this path, I spent 3 months studying at a programming school, where I built my foundational logic using C++ by developing text-based programs like calculators and interactive menus. Later on, driven by personal hobbies, I dove into Java, which expanded my understanding of development. I also explored Python, reaching a similar level of logic with scripts and menus.

Eventually, I discovered HTML & CSS. I instantly fell in love with web development and have been consistently learning, practicing, and refining my front-end skills up to this day.
